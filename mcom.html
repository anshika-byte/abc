About M.Com. Programme


University's online M.Com. programme is ideal for candidates who wish to make a career in Banking, Investment, Equities, Insurance, Financial services as well as Accounting and Commerce. The Program strengthens theoretical, analytical and applied knowledge base of commerce.
The online M.Com. (Master of Commerce ) course is delivered entirely online. The programme is designed to provide students with advanced knowledge and skills in accounting, finance, business management, economics, and other key areas of commerce.
The online M.Com. is suitable for a range of individuals, including working professionals seeking to enhance their skills and knowledge in the field of commerce, recent graduates looking to pursue a career in this field, and entrepreneurs seeking to start or grow their businesses in the commerce sector.
The programme also places strong emphasise on developing key-soft skills such as communication, problem-solving and leadership, which are critical for success.

Benefits of an Online M.Com.

Flexibility: Students can complete coursework at their own pace and on their own schedule, making it easier to balance education with work and family commitments and pursue career, hobbies, studies, all at the same time.

Accessibility: Online M.Com. program can be completed from anywhere in the world through Online classes and recorded lectures making it possible for students to pursue an M.Com. degree without relocating or commuting.

Affordability: Online M.Com. program are often less expensive than traditional on-campus program, making it a more accessible option for students who are concerned about the cost of higher education.

Diverse career opportunities: Online M.Com. program attracts diverse students, including working professionals from a range of industries and geographic locations.

Career advancement: An online M.Com. can help students advance their careers by providing them with the knowledge, skills, and credentials necessary to pursue leadership positions within their organizations.

Networking opportunities: Online M.Com. program offer opportunities to connect with other students, alumni, and industry professionals, providing valuable networking opportunities that can lead to career opportunities and business partnerships.

Industry Collaboration:Online M.Com. Programs an opportunity for practical training in big Corporates for fostering a collaborative learning environment and job opportunities.

Industry readiness program: From the beginning of the final semester, we guide students to be industry ready and for same we mentor them and provide career counselling by experts from all sectors, after interacting effectively, students become future ready professional and thrive at the workplace.

Personal and Professional Growth:Personal and professional growth opportunities prepare students for success in their careers and contribute to their overall development.
