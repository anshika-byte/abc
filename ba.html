About B.A. Programme


University online Bachelor of Arts (B.A. with combination) in Hindi, Political Science, History, Sociology, Economics, English, Philosophy, Public Administration is designed for students looking to gain expertise in various areas and disciplines. This will provide students with broad-based education that focuses on critical thinking, communication and analytical skills. University offers mentorship to help students to reach their full potential and gain the skills and knowledge required to get success.

Our online B.A. program offers a flexible and convenient learning environment that allows students to balance their academic pursuits with work and other responsibilities. Our experienced faculty members deliver high-quality instruction through online class and recorded lectures , interactive sessions and assignments that enable students to apply what they have learned. With access to various online resources and support services, students can maximize their learning experience and prepare themselves for successful careers in various fields of business.

Benefits of an Online B.A.

Flexibility: Students can complete coursework at their own pace and on their own schedule, making it easier to balance education with work and family commitments and pursue career, hobbies, studies, all at the same time.

Accessibility: Online B.A. programs can be completed from anywhere in the world through Online classes and recorded lectures making it possible for students to pursue a B.A. degree without relocating or commuting and prepare for government exams.

Affordability: Online B.A. programs are often less expensive than traditional on-campus programs, making it a more accessible option for students who are concerned about the cost of higher education.

Diverse career opportunities: Online B.A. programs attracts diverse students, including working professionals from a wide range of sectors and geographic locations.

Career advancement: An online B.A. can help students advance their careers by providing them with the knowledge, skills, and credentials necessary to pursue leadership positions within their organizations.

Personal and Professional Growth:Personal and professional growth opportunities prepare students for success in their careers and contribute to their overall development.
